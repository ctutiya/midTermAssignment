Pegasus Values

PLAYER FIRST

SFC's facets of club building, decision making, and recuritement will always revolve around the development of the player first.

LIFESTYLE

SFC promotes not only the lifelong love the game of soccer, yet strives to act as an avenue to healthy living, family values, and success in all aspects of life.

RESPECT

SFC is an ambassador to FIFA RESPECT, we respect ALL; teammates, officials, referees, coaches, opponents, parents, volunteers and ourselves.

EXCELLENCE

SFC excellence pertains to the highest standards of training and competing while also excelling our attitudes, character, sportsmanship and lifestyles.
